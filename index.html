About

My name is Rebeca García Alonso and I am a software and web developer. Since I can remember I have always been using computers and playing videogames. First in my 8086 and my Atari, then in a PC and several generations of consoles. So, long time ago, when I was asked about what I wanted to be in the future, I had the brilliant idea of saying videogame programmer. Few years later I started to study a computer science degree and that was the moment when computer programming captivated me.

After finishing my degree, I have being working in jobs related with programming, developing my skills in several fields of the computer science. I almost touched every aspect of software and web development.

Nowadays I am a passionate programmer who enjoys developing in a lot of different languages even in my free time. I still have the dream of being a videogame developer but I have learnt to love a lot of other aspects related to general programming.
